Run the "setup" file to install SkyDrive Shortcut.

After installation, SkyDrive Shortcut will appear in your taskbar. If you close it, you can find it in your Start menu like any other program.

Click the taskbar icon to open the login screen.

Click the icon again to open the drag-and-drop window.
You can upload files by dragging them into the window (one at a time, or in batches), or by copying and pasting them to this window.
Plain text files can also be created by pasting text into the drag-and-drop window.
To upload screenshots, just press your "print screen" key, open the drag-and-drop-window, and press ctrl+v.
You can also move your mouse to the top-left corner of your screen (even while dragging files) to open the window using a "hot corner".

After dropping or pasting a file, wait a few seconds, and a message will alert you when your upload is finished.

A link will be placed on your clipboard. Paste it in your web browser, or share it with a friend.

SkyDrive Shortcut can be closed by right-clicking the taskbar icon. You can also disable the hot corner from here.
